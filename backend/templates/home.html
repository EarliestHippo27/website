  • Note Ninja

    Note Ninja

    Webapp made for a school group assignment. I was tasked with doing most of the backend, but I also contributes a large portion of the frontend JavaScript. It makes use of Flask and Python for the backend. SQLite for the database. HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery for the frontend. Tokenization is done through itsdangerous. Resetting passwords and sharing documents is done using Flask-Mail.

    To register, your username must be at least 5 characters and your password must be at least 8.

    To login, use your email, not your username.
